Legality

Is playing Img Fantasy legal?

Yes, it is absolutely safe! Img Fantasy is a game of “skill”. The relevant law in India is absolutely clear that, unlike games of "chance", games which substantially require a player to apply his skill are absolutely legal.

All games hosted by Img Fantasy are carefully designed and the jurisdiction of prize winners is strictly monitored to ensure legal compliance with relevant laws currently in force in India.

If you are accessing this site from Assam, Orissa, Telangana, Sikkim or Nagaland you will be prohibited from playing cash contests on Img Fantasy from any of these five states. As the law in these five states is unclear on playing games of skill for cash prizes.
